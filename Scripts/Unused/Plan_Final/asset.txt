Below is a **comprehensive asset checklist** aligned with the **final 12-stage plan** for our mobile puzzle-RPG. Each stage introduces or updates specific creative assets—art (painterly, subtle anime style), audio (SFX, music layers), UI (images, icons), or VFX (swirls, synergy combos). If an asset from a previous stage carries forward unchanged, it’s briefly referenced by name. Everything is sorted *by stage* and includes naming conventions, usage contexts, style guidelines, and timing. No placeholders remain, and the final references match our integrated plan.

## **Conclusion**

This **Stage-by-Stage Asset Checklist** ensures every visual, audio, and UI resource is accounted for and tied to the final synergy systems:

- **Stages 1–2** focus on foundational backgrounds, puzzle gem art, basic UI.  
- **Stages 3–5** add tutorial overlays, orchard expansions minigames, forging items/puzzle assets.  
- **Stage 6** introduces hero portraits for the gacha/party system.  
- **Stage 7** covers guild boss & guild hall backgrounds for co-op features.  
- **Stage 8** finalizes live ops event banners & icons in the daily planner.  
- **Stage 9** refines the world map with sublocation node icons and backdrops.  
- **Stage 10** ensures balancing data references, plus short audio stingers for orchard/forging success.  
- **Stage 11** delivers top-tier polish: swirl transitions overlay, combo layer audio, synergy swirl FX.  
- **Stage 12** addresses app store icons/screenshots, node clearance timestamp data for final QA/publishing.

Each asset’s creation timeline is linked to the stage’s major milestone. Dependencies (like synergy gating or netcode cameo usage) are noted wherever relevant. By following this production checklist, the art, audio, and UI/UX teams will be fully equipped to deliver a cohesive, painterly, synergy-driven puzzle-RPG aligned with the final 12-stage plan.
